The Event

The City of London has decided to organise and host an event for students from different universities within the city.

In this event, students can chose to walk, cycle or run the 10 mile route that has been chosen by the city, this route is based on the same route as the London Marathon.

There will have regular checkpoints along the route, so that if a student cannot walk for any longer, they will be able to simply drop out of marathon at those checkpoints. Students also have a choice of attending this event individually or in groups.

This event is done with the partnership with Adidas and will be held on the 28th of June 2020.

The Aim

Currently students carry lot of responsibilities on themselves: they are attending classes, attending extracurricular activities, studying, preparing for exams, balancing their social life and working part time jobs.

Students can easily become stressed from the amount of work that they have to do, which can affect their mental health and wellbeing. When a student is stressed, they might find difficulties in focusing on tasks, irritated and be anxious about things.

If a student is stressed for a long period of time they might become depressed. This is why students need a way to release that stress and just relax.

This event was organised so that students can relax from their responsibilities and socialise with other students whilst exercising, which can be beneficial for their physical and mental health.

It is also a good way for new students who are considering studying in London to get familiar with the city.

This event was made so that it would encourage students to take part in physical exercise more often and to try to get these students interested in doing other sports.

The Route

The walk will begin at Gower Street, which is in front of the University of London at 11:00 am. After 1.6 miles, students will walk past by City, University of London and enter into St John Street. At 2.7 miles, student will pass St. Paul Cathedral and enter into Cannon Street. At 3.1 miles, after going through Eastcheap, the student will walk by the Tower of London and will walk onto the Tower Bridge. The students will continue walking on Tower Bridge Road until they reach the train viaduct, then they will turn into Druid Street and continue. From Crucifix Lane, they will pass to Snowfields and continue walking straight into Newcomen Street. The student will then head left when entering Borough High Street and enter Borough Street shortly after. Students will then cross over to Westminster Bridge Road in order to go on the Westminster Bridge. After going over Westminster Bridge, student will head down the Victoria Embankment and turn towards Northumberland Avenue. Student will then be able to enter The Mall and walk past by the Buckingham Palace and walk on to Constitution Hill. The participant will walk under the Wellington Arch on Aspley Way and will then head onto Knightsbridge and on Kensington Road. The students will then head in to Hyde Park, which is where the finish will be.

Details

This event is expected to be finished at 4 pm.

The total length of the route is 10 miles or 16km.

Refreshment points will be present every two miles on the route, in which participants can consume refreshment drinks or drop out.

Toilets will also be present if needed.

Participants must also have one item of adidas apparel on them during the walk.
